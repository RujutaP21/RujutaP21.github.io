Design Service for Skin focused people

Design Goal

Use of GPS to detect weather and pollution of the user’s location.
The AI to identify the possible factors that are affecting the user’s skin and provides the best possible solution to repair or prevent damages.

Strategy Proposal

BeautyMask is the only personal assistant skill that gives you personalized face mask ingredients for SKIN FOCUSED PEOPLE

User Research questions

  1. General Information
    • Name
    • Age
    • Occupation
    • Region

  2. Skincare Information
    • How do you take care about your skin?
    • Do you have any skincare routine? (Usually have a daily skincare routine)
      And how frequently?
    • do you take care of you skin from outer weather or pollution? (Yes)

  3. Skincare products information
    • What kind of products you use for your skin?
    • What factors do you keep in mind while buying skin products?
    • Do you take any expert advise before buying skin products?

  4. Habits information
    • How much water do you drink for day? (Usually more then 2 liter)
    • Do you eat green vegetables and fruits? (Usually a lot)
    • How long do you sleep for night? (Usually more then 7 hours)
